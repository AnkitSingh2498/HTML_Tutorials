Audio Tag

The <audio> tag in HTML is used to embed sound content like music or sound effects on a web page.

Attributes:
    * controls: Shows the built-in play/pause/volume controls.
    * autoplay: Audio starts playing automatically (may be muted by default).
    * muted: Mutes the audio.
    * loop: Audio will restart automatically when it ends.
    * preload: Hints to the browser how to load the audio (auto, metadata, none).
