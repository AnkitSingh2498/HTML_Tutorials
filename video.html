Video Tag

The <video> tag in HTML is used to embed video content on a web page. It's a container for video playback, often with controls like play, pause, and volume.

Attributes:
    * controls: Displays playback controls.
    * autoplay: Starts playing the video automatically (may be muted by default in some browsers).
    * muted: Mutes the video.
    * loop: Repeats the video when it ends.
    * poster: Specifies an image to show before the video starts.
